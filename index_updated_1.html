About me

I'm Hariesh Kumar S K, a Senior Generative AI Engineer with 3.5+ years of hands-on experience designing and deploying enterprise-grade AI systems — from multi-repository knowledge bots and autonomous multi-agent workflows to governed LLM pipelines built on GCP

My path into AI was deliberate. Starting in QA engineering, I developed a sharp eye for reliability, system behavior, and production quality — instincts that now directly shape how I architect AI systems. I don't just build AI that works in a demo. I build AI that holds up in production: grounded, governed, auditable, and safe.

At Prodapt, I led development of a multi-repository telecom knowledge assistant integrating SharePoint, Guided action for field technicians — earning the Rising Rookie Award. At Cognizant, I built RAG-based chatbots, drove GenAI knowledge-sharing sessions, and earned the Spotlight Award.

Today I specialize in LLM engineering, RAG systems, AI agents, LangGraph workflows, MCP integrations, and Responsible AI governance. I also mentor developers transitioning into AI and helping on their first production AI systems.
