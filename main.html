About me

I'm Hariesh Kumar S K, a Senior Generative AI Engineer with 3+ years of hands-on experience designing and deploying enterprise-grade AI systems — from multi-repository knowledge bots and autonomous multi-agent telecom workflows to governed LLM pipelines built on GCP and Azure.

My path into AI was deliberate. Starting in QA engineering, I developed a sharp eye for reliability, system behavior, and production quality — instincts that now directly shape how I architect AI systems. I don't just build AI that works in a demo. I build AI that holds up in production: grounded, governed, auditable, and safe.

At Prodapt, I led development of a multi-repository telecom knowledge assistant integrating Jira, SharePoint, Git, ServiceNow, and Watson — earning the Rising Rookie Award. At Cognizant, I built RAG-based chatbots, drove GenAI knowledge-sharing sessions, and earned the Spotlight Award.

Today I specialize in LLM engineering, RAG systems, AI agents, LangGraph workflows, MCP integrations, and Responsible AI governance. I also mentor developers transitioning into AI and consult with startups on their first production AI systems.

Responsible AI & governance

Building powerful AI is only half the challenge. At enterprise scale, AI systems must also be explainable, auditable, safe to operate autonomously, and compliant with organizational risk standards.

Across every system I've deployed at Prodapt and Cognizant, Responsible AI was not an afterthought — it was a design constraint from day one. I build AI that leadership can demo, compliance teams can audit, and operations can trust to run at scale.
